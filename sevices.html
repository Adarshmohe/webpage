Our Services

Road Transport:
Includes trucking, delivery services, and passenger transportation.

Rail Transport:
Utilizes trains for both freight and passenger movement, often for long distances.

Air Transport:
Offers rapid transportation of goods and passengers, particularly suitable for time-sensitive deliveries.

Maritime/Sea Transport:
Involves shipping goods across oceans and waterways, ideal for large-scale cargo.

Bus Rapid Transit (BRT):
A high capacity public transportation system using buses for efficient passenger movement.

History :

Indore's transportation history, featuring public buses, radio taxis, and e-bikes, began with the incorporation of Indore City Transport Services Limited in 2005, a PPP scheme operating under Atal Indore City Transport Services Ltd, now operating on 36 routes and 170 bus stops.
